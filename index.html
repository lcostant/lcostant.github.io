Junior Leader Fellow "la Caixa"

Astrobiology Center (CAB/INTA-CSIC)

Madrid, Spain

I am an observational astrophysicist studying the formation and evolution of galaxies. I am particularly interested in the physical mechanisms which govern the morphological buildup of galaxies and the co-evolution of their structural components (bulges, disks, bars) across cosmic time.

I am the PI of the OMEGA (Origin of the Morphological Evolution of GAlaxies) project and member of 11 international collaborations, including the JWST-MIRI Guaranteed Team (European Consortium) and the Cosmic Evolution Early Release Science survey (JWST-CEERS).
