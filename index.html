Atracción de Talento

Astrobiology Center (CAB/INTA-CSIC)

Madrid, Spain

I am an observational astrophysicist studying the formation and evolution of galaxies. I am particularly interested in the physical mechanisms which govern the morphological buildup of galaxies and the co-evolution of their structural components (bulges, disks, bars) across cosmic time.

I am part of 10 international collaborations, including the JWST-MIRI Guaranteed Team, the Cosmic Evolution Early Release Science survey (CEERS), the Survey for High-z Absorption Red and Dead Sources (SHARDS), the Stellar Populations at intermediate redshifts Survey (StePS), and the Bulgeless Evolution and the Rise of Disks survey (BEARD).
